About us

Authentic Neapolitan tradition, served with style

Sergio & Robi's Pizzeria brings together authentic Italian flavour, live wood fired cooking and warm hospitality to create a food experience your guests will truly remember.

Wood Fired Pizza • Mobile Catering • Event Hire

Founded by Italian chefs from Naples, the birthplace of pizza, Sergio & Robi's Pizzeria is built on tradition, quality ingredients and decades of real experience.

With over 30 years of experience, we specialise in bringing authentic Neapolitan pizza to weddings, private parties, corporate events and special occasions across Northampton and the surrounding areas.

Our service is about more than just food — it is about atmosphere, presentation and giving your guests something that feels personal, generous and genuinely memorable.
